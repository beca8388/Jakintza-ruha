What is Jakintza Ruha?

Jakintza Ruha is a spiritual, ancestral, and educational movement devoted to remembering ancient truths, reclaiming indigenous wisdom, and restoring the sacred rhythm of life. It is not a religion—it is a return. A return to the stars, to the Earth, and to the memory carried in our bones.

Our Mission

  • 🌿 Reawaken ancient knowledge through language, ritual, and ceremony
  • 🔥 Guide individuals through elemental mastery and ascension practices
  • 🌕 Integrate astrology, herbs, sacred geometry, and story into modern education
  • 🌎 Build trilingual schools rooted in English, Español, and Nahuatl

We are the bridge between forgotten ages and a future worth remembering. Jakintza Ruha is a sanctuary for those who are ready to unlearn, remember, and rise.

— Aion Itzuli, Founder
